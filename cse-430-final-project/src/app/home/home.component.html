Climbing Tracker

A Route Tracker for Climbers

The purpose of this app is the help climbers track their climbing progression and compare with their stats with friends and others

You can see your progress in the "My Stuff" tab. It is a list of all the accomplishments you have documented in this app

You can see your friends progress in the "Friends" tab. It is a list of all the accomplishments you and your friends have documented in this app
